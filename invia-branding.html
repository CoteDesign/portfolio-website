INVIA BRAND & CONTENT

Branding, Content Creation, & Web

INVIA is a medical software company that produces an innovative nuclear cardiac imaging software for medical professionals to review and report their findings efficiently. As a young company with big medical terms and a bare minimum look, I decided to refresh the brand to a clean, modern feel. With clients in B2B and B2C, I wanted to present their wealth of helpful resources in a fun, professional, and easy to understand way with their website, brochures, and videos. For this I was the lead Graphic Designer working as a team with the Marketing Manager.

VIDEOS

INVIA wanted more videos to accomodate the clients that needed quick how-to instructions and education on a certain product feature. I started the Education Shorts line of videos providing a quick one minute explanation of a topic aided by simple animations. These videos were written, illustrated, and animated by me.

CONFERENCE BOOTH

Every year INVIA had a booth showcased at five major scientific conferences. For the international conference booths I would design graphics to print on the booth walls. Depending on the type of conference we would select a specific feature of the 4DM software to advertise.

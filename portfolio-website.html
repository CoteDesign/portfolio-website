HELLO MY NAME IS CHANTEL COTE.

I’m a graphic designer with an interest in creating the most memorable, captivating, informative, and intuitive experiences.

Joining together the expectations of the client with the needs of the user is my ultimate goal as a graphic designer. The challenge of finding the most effective and visually appealing way to present the information is one that I love to take on. For fives years, I’ve been helping companies build a stronger presence through creative content, consistent visual identity, and easy to use solutions. I’m also skilled in user interface design, event design, and illustration.
